## Chosen Combination (one-line summary for 46.1-08)

**Apply candidate (a) `<table bgcolor>` body wrapper + candidate (b) `@media (prefers-color-scheme: dark)` block + candidate (d) inline body-attribute paint on top of the existing 46.1-04 `[data-ogsc]`/`[data-ogsb]`/MSO conditional lock in `src/emails/shell/EmailShell.tsx`.**
